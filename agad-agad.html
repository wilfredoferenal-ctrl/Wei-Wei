Mt.

Agad

Nestled in Iligan City, Mount Agad-Agad is a popular attraction for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers. Rising 1,650 feet above sea level, it offers an exhilarating hiking experience with stunning vistas of Iligan Bay and the city. The mountain is known for its captivating sunrise treks and mesmerizing night views of the city lights.
