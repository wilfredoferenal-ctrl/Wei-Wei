ILIGAN CITY

Centennial
Park

Dalipuga Centennial Park in Iligan City is a picturesque haven for tourists seeking relaxation and natural beauty. This expansive park offers lush greenery, scenic walking paths, and tranquil spots perfect for unwinding or enjoying a picnic with friends and family. Open from early morning to late evening, it's a must-visit attraction for anyone exploring the area.
